Badges

Badges are being used to display a notification count or status information

Examples of Status Badges

We have 2 types of status badges that can be integrated with Avatars.You can use colors to show the online status of user.
Add class avatars avatar-lg status and according to status busy_status active_status etc to add these in your project.

Examples of text-Badges

We have 2 types of text badges that can be integrated with text. Badges with rounded edges and square edges.
Add classes text-badge1 and for round badge badge_round to add these in your projects.
